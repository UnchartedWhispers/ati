Artificial Tripartite Intelligence

A Bio-Inspired Sensor-First Framework for Physical AI
Paper Demo Video Code Poster
Key idea. Artificial Tripartite Intelligence treats sensing as an active part of physical AI, coordinating sensor control, local inference, and selective escalation to improve robustness under difficult capture conditions.

Overview

Physical AI systems often focus on model-side adaptation while treating sensors as passive inputs. ATI instead places sensing control at the front of the loop, allowing the system to improve what it captures before escalating to larger remote models.
